Features

Everything you need to
run a broadcast

From plug-in to live stream in under a minute. No cloud accounts, no monthly fees.

Real-Time Updates

Every move broadcasts instantly via WebSocket. Spectators see moves, clocks, and evaluations the moment they happen on the physical board.

Up to 20 Boards

Run a full congress or major event. Every board gets its own live feed, evaluation bar, and clock display.

Stockfish Analysis

Built-in engine evaluations with multi-PV lines, win probability, and per-player accuracy percentages.

Spectator Links

Share a URL or QR code. Anyone on the network can watch the broadcast live in their browser. No app needed.

Fully Offline

Runs entirely on your local machine. No cloud dependency, no internet required. Your data stays yours.

Tournament Management

Create round-robin, Swiss, or congress tournaments with automatic pairings. Set up players, rounds, and time controls through a built-in wizard. PGN files are generated and managed automatically.

$ tournament create --format swiss --players 20
Generating 5 rounds of pairings...
Tournament "Spring Open" created. 10 boards ready.

Live Clocks

DGT clock times displayed and countdown between updates for smooth, accurate time displays.

OBS Integration

Dedicated OBS browser source widgets for individual boards, standings ticker, and leaderboards.

Setup

Three steps to
go live

01

Install & Launch

Download the app for your platform. Open it. The server starts automatically — no terminal, no configuration files.

02

Connect Boards

Point to your DGT LiveChess output folder, or create a tournament and let the simulator generate test games.

03

Go Live

Click "Open Live View" and share the spectator link. Every connected browser sees moves in real-time.
